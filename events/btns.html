Rules

  • TEAM SIZE: 1
  • The submission should be in the form of a short story.
  • The story should involve a minimum of three characters.
  • The participants are being provided with an extract. It's mandatory to include the extract in it's exact form in the story.
  • The submission should not exceed 1500 words.
  • Plagiarism in any form shall lead to immediate disqualification of the submitted work.
  • The decision of the Judges and the organizing committee will be final and binding.
