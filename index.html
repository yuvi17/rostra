ROSTRA

Random set of changes will be made through this.Rostra is to be held between 12th to 14th of February, 2016, to recognize the stellar debater, the double-quick thinker, and the crowd wooer in you. As an inter-college national level conjuncture, it hubs both online and offline events with prizes worth thousands. Organized by the Literary Society of BIT Mesra, Rostra promises to offer appetizing competitions in the field of your passion. It commences beyond convention, raises the bar for the adroit and pushes the bar of what is top notch.



LITERARY SOCIETY

We, the Literary Society, BIT Mesra, work towards establishing highest standards for those who aim to be a part of a unique literary experience. We endeavour to provide the most challenging and engrossing competitions for the students to participate in, and the response thus received keeps the adrenaline pumped to help us reiterate the competitions with greater boom each time, mixing them with a zing of novelty and innovation.

Rules

  • Participants may come in teams or they may register as an individual on the official website. A team shall consist of a maximum of 5 participants.
  • Registrations begin from the 4th of February, 2016.
  • As per the time of registration, participants will be divided into two subgroups based on which separate charges will apply: Early Wingers (those who apply before the 7th February, 2016) Late Wingers (those who apply after the 7th February, 2016)
  • Each team can reserve at least one participant for the event YOUTH-PARLIAMENT as per their liking. Youth Parliament will start on the 13th of February and will continue throughout the next day as well.
  • For individual events, a team can have only one of the members to represent the team. The other members of the team, if they wish to participate, shall have to register individually for the particular event.
    Registrations for all formal & Informal events:
    Open- 4th February, 2016
    Close- 12th February, 2016
  • Registrations for Youth Parliament :
    Open- 7th February, 2016
    Close- 12th February, 2016
  • Registrations for online submissions:
    'Be the Next Shakespeare' (national level):
    Open- 7th February, 2016
    Close- 14th February, 2016
  • 'Rewrite the History' and 'Shabd Dwand' :
    Open- 7th February 2016
    Close- 14th February, 2016
  • The members of the participating teams may have to make certain arrangements according to the schedule of the simultaneous events. Kindly decide your team as per the event schedule.

Accomodation Policy

  1. Everyone willing to take accommodation for Rostra'16 must register on the website
  2. When you arrive at BIT Mesra, you need to come to the Info Cell along with your college identity card. You will be allotted your place of stay on campus. Accommodation would be strictly on shared basis.
  3. In case of any disputes, decision of Hospitality CGs would be final and binding.
  • Charges will apply as per the following:
    {these hold true only for those who are not currently studying at BIT Mesra, Ranchi.}
    For the Early Wingers:
    Participation in all the events (except Youth Parliament) : Rs. 100/- per individual.
    Participation in all the events with Accommodation (except Youth Parliament): Rs. 100/- + Rs. 150/- per individual.
    Participation in Youth Parliament: Rs. 100/- per individual.
    Participation in Youth parliament with Accommodation: Rs.100/- + Rs.150/- per individual.
  • For the Late Wingers:
    Participation in all the events (except Youth Parliament): Rs. 100/- per individual.
    Participation in all the events with Accommodation (except Youth Parliament): Rs. 100/- + Rs. 200/- per individual.
    Participation in Youth Parliament: Rs. 100/- per individual.
    Participation in Youth parliament with Accommodation: Rs.100/- + Rs. 200/- per individual.

*The charges will be the same irrespective of duration of stay up to a maximum of four days i.e. from the 12th of February to the 16th of February (up till 10 am ), 2016. Due to limited availability of space, accommodation will be provided to participants on a preferential basis.

Some Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q1. I want to take part in Rostra 2016. What are the steps I should follow?
    Step 1: Register in the website for Rostra 2016.
    Step 2: Fill in the details as per given in the form and submit.
    Step 3: Voila! You are registered for Rostra.
  • Q2. Do I have to pay any registration fee/accommodation fee for Rostra?
    Yes, you do. Refer to the Accommodation Policy mentioned before.
  • Q3. Can I register even if I don't have a team?
    Yes, we allow single participation.
  • Q4. What is the maximum size of a team?
    A team should not have more than 5 members.
  • Q5. Can the team have participants from different colleges/school?
    Yes, a team can have members from different colleges/schools.
  • Q6. What are Early Wingers and Late Wingers?
    Participants who register before the 7th February, 2016 will be regarded as the Early Wingers while those who register after the 7th February, 2016 will considered the Late Wingers.
  • Q7. What type of accommodation will be provided?
    Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well-guarded separate hostels on the campus of BIT Mesra.
  • Q7. What type of accommodation will be provided?
    Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well-guarded separate hostels on the campus of BIT Mesra.
  • Q8. Are separate arrangements made for accommodation of professors?
    Separate accommodation arrangements for faculty are subjected to availability and cannot be guaranteed.
  • Q9. One of my queries has not been answered. What should I do?
    No worries. Just drop in a mail at rostra16@gmail.com You can also get in touch with the Hospitality Team.
